Euro Cup Voucher Campaign ROI Analysis

Marketing ROI

Question

Did the Euro Cup voucher campaign generate positive returns? How did conversion differ across player segments and providers?

Method

Segmented conversion analysis by player type (new/repeat/return) and provider; ROI calculation; post-event return timing and betting day tracking.

Finding

ROI = 4.54 · 20% of voucher users returned post-event · avg. return: Day 5.64

Impact

Day 5–6 re-engagement notification and extra rewards for players betting 4+ days adopted into subsequent marketing strategy.

B2B Active Player Churn Investigation

Churn Diagnosis

Question

Why did active B2B players drop significantly in the THB and IDR markets? Are the churn drivers market-specific?

Method

Analyzed player login timing, Bet Credit structure, and agent activity status per SMA, cross-referenced by market and audit group.

Finding

Two churn profiles: "has balance, not playing" vs "wants to play, no balance" · Suspicious agent inactivity flagged in audit groups zad & hkq

Impact

Differentiated re-engagement strategies per churn profile; suspicious audit groups flagged for further investigation.

Game Lobby UX Funnel Analysis

Product Funnel

Question

After launching the new game lobby, why were players struggling to find slot games? Where were users dropping off?

Method

Built a full browsing funnel tracking scroll and click rates at each lobby section; analyzed navigation paths of users who stopped scrolling.

Finding

Three major drop points: Slots→Category (-27%) · Category→Provider Banner (-19%) · Provider Banner→Provider Card (-48%)

Impact

Product team adjusted lobby architecture based on analysis — promotion entry earlier in flow, games embedded directly in categories.

Email Campaign Causal Analysis

Causal Inference

Question

Does the Cashbox prize notification email actually drive player betting behavior? Do readers outperform non-readers?

Method

Between-group comparison (readers vs. non-readers); within-group peak performance timing analysis; turnover trend before and after reading.

Finding

Readers outperformed non-readers — but no sufficient statistical evidence of causality. Correlation, not causation.

Impact

Email repositioned away from primary turnover driver; channel investment and messaging strategy redesigned accordingly.

VIP Player Behavioral Profiling

User Profiling

Question

What behavioral patterns define a newly identified high-potential player? How does their engagement compare to existing VIP benchmarks?

Method

Analyzed active days from registration, provider and game distribution, turnover and win/loss trends; benchmarked against existing VIP player.

Finding

Higher engagement in even months · Strong loyalty to favorite game · Stickiness and bet size exceed VIP benchmark at comparable stage

Impact

Personalized game recommendations (Bonanza series, high-volatility games) and even-month promotions adopted into recommendation strategy.

Cashbox Promotion Feature Performance

Feature Analytics

Question

How did the Cashbox feature perform post-launch on ROI? Did prize distribution follow design rules? Did player turnover increase?

Method

Tracked daily prize distribution and achievement rates; calculated ROI; analyzed turnover changes for prize winners; validated prize probability distribution against 40/40/20 design spec.

Finding

141 players won prizes in 6 days · Prizes = 4.9% of net winnings · Distribution matched 40/40/20 spec · Winners showed turnover growth

Impact

Current settings maintained for two-week observation; Mystery Mode proposed for players completing all prizes to sustain engagement.

Game Provider Preference Segmentation

Segmentation

Question

What proportion of players concentrate on only a few providers? Are there substitution patterns between providers?

Method

Segmented players by number of providers played (1/2/multiple); analyzed provider distribution per segment; deep-dived into substitution preferences among players avoiding mainstream providers.

Finding

76% of players (91,741) played only 1–2 providers · 80% of non-mainstream players concentrated on a single alternative provider

Impact

Findings informed provider partnership prioritization and personalized recommendation strategy; targeted promotions by provider preference profile recommended.
